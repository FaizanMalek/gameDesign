How the math works. A blackjack deck has known odds for every card, so the value of each decision can be computed exactly. The two rules that most help the player are built in: a natural blackjack pays 3 to 2, and the dealer must stand on 17. The dealer busts about 28% of the time, which is where most of the player's value comes from. Full blackjack with splitting and basic strategy returns about 99.5%. This casual build leaves splitting out to stay simple, so its house edge sits near 1%. The skill is in when to hit, stand, or double.
