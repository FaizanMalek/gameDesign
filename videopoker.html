How the math works. The deck has 52 cards, so every five card hand has a known probability. The 9/6 name means a full house pays 9 and a flush pays 6, the two values that most affect the payback. Multiply each hand's pay by its chance of occurring with correct holding decisions and the whole game returns 99.54%, one of the highest in any casino. The skill is in which cards you hold, which is where strategy and math meet.
