How to play (read me once)

  1. Press Spin. Your bet (tot 20) leaves your balance; any win is added back.
  2. You win when the same symbol lands on a line, reading left to right, starting at column 1. The lines are not just straight rows: they zigzag. The game now draws the winning line in fire so you can see it.
  3. The match must start at the far-left column. Three matching symbols in columns 2-3-4 do not pay, because they skip column 1. This is why some "three in a row" you spot won't win.
  4. The Crown (♛) is wild and fills in for any symbol, so a winning line can include a Crown.
  5. 3+ crossed swords (⚔) anywhere trigger the Boss Bonus: 8 free spins at double pay.

Wild, the Ashen Crown (♛) substitutes for every symbol except the Boss Sigil and carries the top line pay itself. 3 or more Boss Sigils anywhere ignite the Boss Bonus: 8 free spins at a 2× win multiplier, retriggered by landing 3+ Sigils again. Wins pay left to right on 20 fixed lines, highest win per line only. Runes (ᚠ ᚨ ᛒ ᛁ) are the common low pays; Bonfire, Hollow King, Cleric and Estus are the premiums.

Design intent: the high σ is deliberate. A Souls game is about long droughts punctuated by a brutal boss; the paytable concentrates return into the rare Boss Bonus and 5 of a kind premiums rather than frequent small wins, which is what the volatility number proves. Lowering the Bonfire and Crown 5 of a kind pays would flatten σ and make the game "smoother" at the same RTP, the kind of lever a designer pulls to fit a target player.
